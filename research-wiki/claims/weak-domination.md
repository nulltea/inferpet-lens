# Bayes-optimal estimator weakly dominates lossy/linear attack

**status:** `drafted`

## Statement
For fixed joint law, Bayes risk using a coarsened statistic T(X) (lossy RowSort phi, or noise-naive linear ridge) >= Bayes risk of the Bayes-optimal estimator on X (Blackwell post-processing; Rao-Blackwell under convex loss; MI-DPI).

## Honest scope
Weak (>=) domination holds for all priors/losses. Noise-distribution agnostic (Gaussian AND Laplace).
